About

Our project aim is to promote sustainable waste management by incentivizing the collection of plastic waste and identifying its type. By using an IR sensor and an Arduino board, our system can detect the type of plastic, and the collected data will be stored in a database. Through this initiative, we aim to reduce the amount of plastic waste in the environment and encourage individuals to be more environmentally conscious.
The collected data can also be used to further research and develop better waste management practices.
